About

Recently, there has been a sharp rise in proposed legislation aimed specifically at the transgender community across almost all states in the US. This legislation can take the form of restricting transgender existence in public (ie. anything from the banning of drag shows, to the criminalization of transgender people using the bathroom of their true gender, to the broad banning of ‚Äúcross-sex‚Äù dress and mannerisms in public), or increased restrictions on the process of transition (ie. anything from the banning of discussion of transgender topics in schools to the banning of medical transition-related procedures such as HRT and gender-affirming surgeries). Most of this sort of proposed legislation has been on the state level. While most of these proposed laws do not pass, the ones that do make life more difficult for transgender people living in said state.
